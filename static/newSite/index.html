About Us

We are curious and motivated students. Our aim is developing a personalized software revolving around face accessories.

  • Located in Istanbul, our members share a common delight of innovation and development. We as engineering students want to create new products and develop existing ones.


  • Starring off as a hackathon team, our ambition and ideas grew stronger with each day. We as a team participated in 3 hackathons and won 2 awards. Which made us keen on trying harder every time, thus our desire to grow is persisted.

Our History

May 2019

Cozone Energy Efficiency Hackathon

In this hackathon based on energy efficiency, we came up with a gamified platform for tracking the energy consumption and comparing it to their surroundings.

April 2019

Pixery Hackathon

The Pixery hackathon is where the seeds of this project were planted. We developed an app which would suggest glasses to users according to their facial features.

December 2018

İTÜ BEST Hackathon

The idea of this hackathon was to create an IoT platform to solve a problem in kitchens. We built a smart fridge which could keep track of what is inside.
